About the Beagle

The Beagle is a small to medium-sized hound known for its curious and friendly nature.

Style: Curious, Affectionate, Vocal.

Often used as detection dogs due to their strong sense of smell.

Details
  1. Origin: England
  2. Size: Small to Medium
  3. Life Expectancy: 12-15 years
