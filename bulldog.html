About the Bulldog

Bulldogs are medium-sized, muscular dogs known for their loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose.

Personality: Calm, Courageous, Loyal.

Bulldogs are great family pets and adapt well to apartment living.

Facts
  • Size: Medium
  • Life Expectancy: 8-10 years
  • Temperament: Gentle
